The Church Fathers

The bishops, theologians, and spiritual guides whose writings define Orthodox doctrine and practice — from the Apostolic era to the fall of Constantinople.

The Church Fathers are not historical curiosities — they are living voices of the Church, still quoted in every Divine Liturgy, still read by every catechumen, still cited in every theological dispute. To know Orthodoxy is to know these men: their struggles, their precision, and their love for the God they spent their lives trying to describe.
