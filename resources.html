Books & Resources

Resources for the Journey

These resources are offered to help you begin, grow, and go deeper in the life of the Church. Whether you are new to Orthodoxy, preparing for reception, raising children in the faith, or seeking deeper study — this library is meant to be a trustworthy companion for the journey.

This library is organized into five categories, each moving from Inquirer through Catechumen to The Faithful. Browse freely — or if you are new to the faith, begin anywhere marked Inquirer.
