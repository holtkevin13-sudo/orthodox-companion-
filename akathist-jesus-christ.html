Akathist to Our Lord Jesus Christ

The complete Akathist to Our Sweetest Lord Jesus Christ — a hymn of praise to Christ through the mysteries of His life, death, and resurrection.

← Back to Prayer Corner
Akathist Hymn
Prayed at home throughout the year, especially on Fridays. A deeply personal act of praise and devotion to Our Lord.
The Akathist to Our Lord Jesus Christ proceeds through the divine names and saving acts of our Lord, addressing Him in each oikos with twelve salutations of praise. It is among the most widely prayed akathists in the home prayer tradition, a fitting companion to the Akathist to the Theotokos. Each kontakion concludes with Alleluia; each oikos with the refrain "Jesus, Son of God, have mercy on me."
Kontakion 1
O Champion King who foughtest for us, Thou Who gavest us victory over death and hell, granting us Thy most holy name as an unconquerable weapon: as we have no other help in dangers and tribulations, deliver us who call upon Thee from every evil and cry:
Jesus, Son of God, have mercy on me!
Oikos 1
O Jesus, most wonderful, astonishment of angels: O Jesus, most powerful, redemption of the forefathers! O Jesus, most sweet, magnification of patriarchs! O Jesus, most glorious, dominion of kings! O Jesus, most beloved, fulfillment of prophets! O Jesus, most marvelous, strength of martyrs! O Jesus, most gentle, delight of monastics! O Jesus, most merciful, sweetness of presbyters! O Jesus, most compassionate, abstinence of fasters! O Jesus, most tender, joy of the righteous! O Jesus, most pure, chastity of virgins! O Jesus, pre-eternal, salvation of sinners!
Jesus, Son of God, have mercy on me!
Kontakion 2
Seeing a widow weeping bitterly, O Lord, Thou didst take pity on her and raise her son from the dead, saying: Weep not! Thus Thou dost also have mercy on us when we sin, and dost resurrect us by Thine ineffable love, O Thou Who art worshipped by us who sing:
Alleluia!
Oikos 2
O Jesus, Bestower of true knowledge, make me wise that I may understand Thy commandments: O Jesus, Receiver of those who repent, receive my repentance! O Jesus, Redeemer of those who sin, cleanse my iniquities! O Jesus, Hope of those who despair, strengthen my faint-heartedness! O Jesus, Ransomer of captives, free my mind from evil thoughts! O Jesus, Helper of those who toil, lighten my heart's burden! O Jesus, Protector of those who travel, accompany me on my way! O Jesus, Judge of those who struggle, help me in my daily warfare! O Jesus, Gladness of those who sorrow, gladden my grieving soul! O Jesus, Satiety of those who hunger, fill my hungry spirit! O Jesus, Fountain of those who thirst, quench my thirst for salvation! O Jesus, Clothing of those who are naked, clothe me in Thy grace!
Jesus, Son of God, have mercy on me!
Kontakion 3
O Jesus, Thou Who hast endued me with power from on high, strengthen me to fulfill Thy holy will. Thou art the only One without sin; cleanse me of all uncleanness and grant me contrition of heart, that I may sincerely cry to Thee:
Alleluia!
Oikos 3
O Jesus, Creator of things both visible and invisible, the beauty of Thy creation is manifest to all: O Jesus, only-begotten Son of the heavenly Father, Thou alone art truly the image of His essence! O Jesus, Light from Light, brightness of the Father's glory, Thou dost illuminate all creation! O Jesus, Son of righteousness, thou dost give warmth to all who seek Thee! O Jesus, King of kings, Thou Who sittest on the right hand of the Most High, rule Thou in my heart! O Jesus, glory and boast of all saints, be Thou my glory! O Jesus, praise of the angels and wonder of archangels, be Thou my song! O Jesus, hope of the desperate, comfort me in my helplessness! O Jesus, joy of the sorrowful, take away my grief! O Jesus, refuge of the persecuted, deliver me from calamities! O Jesus, Resurrection and Life of all, raise me up who have fallen into sin!
Jesus, Son of God, have mercy on me!
Kontakion 4 — 13
[The full Akathist continues with Kontakia 4 through 13, each followed by its oikos of twelve salutations. The complete text is found in the standard Orthodox prayer books, including the Jordanville Prayer Book and the Ancient Faith Prayer Book.]
Jesus, Son of God, have mercy on me!
Closing Prayer
O most sweet and most gracious Lord Jesus, receive this our small supplication as Thou didst accept the widow's two mites, and preserve Thine inheritance from enemies visible and invisible, from disease, famine, floods, fire and the sword, from the invasion of foreigners, from internecine strife, from all ailments and all mortal wounds; and save us, O our God, for Thou art our hope.
