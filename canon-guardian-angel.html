Canon to the Guardian Angel

The Canon to the Guardian Angel — the third of the three Pre-Communion canons, also prayed independently on Mondays and in times of spiritual danger.

← Back to Prayer Corner
Canon of Intercession
The third of the three Pre-Communion canons, read the evening before Holy Communion. Also prayed on Mondays (the day of the Angels) and in times of spiritual need.
This canon is addressed to the Guardian Angel — the heavenly companion appointed to each soul at baptism to guard, intercede, and accompany it through life and at the hour of death. As the third of the three Pre-Communion canons, it is read after the Canon of Repentance and the Canon to the Theotokos. It is also prayed independently as an evening prayer, particularly on Mondays when the Orthodox Church commemorates the Angelic Powers.
Before the Canon
Blessed is our God, always, now and ever and unto the ages of ages. Amen. Glory to Thee, our God, glory to Thee.
Ode 1 — Irmos
Having traversed the depth of the Red Sea dryshod, the Israel of old, with arms extended in the form of the Cross, put to flight the power of Amalek in the wilderness.
Ode 1 — Troparia
O holy Angel, Guardian of my soul and body, forgive me all that I have offended thee by, and whatever I have sinned this day; deliver me from the crafty snares of the enemy, that I may not anger my God. O holy Guardian of my soul, pray for me and intercede with the Creator of all, that with thee I may stand in the light of His countenance at the Last Day. Holy Angel, who standest before the throne of the Holy Trinity, intercede for me who am condemned by many sins, that I perish not, but that I find favor before the dread Judge.
Kontakion
O holy Angel who guardest my wretched soul and passionate life, forsake me not, a sinner, nor depart from me for my licentiousness. Leave no room for the evil demon to gain dominion over me through the oppression of this mortal body; but take me by my decrepit and outstretched hand, and lead me in the way of salvation. Yea, O holy Angel of God, the guardian and protector of my wretched soul and body, forgive me everything in which I have offended thee all the days of my life, and whatever I have sinned this past night. Protect me in the present day and preserve me from every temptation of the enemy, that I may not anger God by any sin; and intercede for me before the Lord, that He might confirm me in His fear and make me a worthy servant of His goodness. Amen.
Ode 3 — Irmos
Heaven's vault was established by no other than Thine all-powerful Word, O Christ, and the Church was founded upon the rock of Thy commandments. Holy Lord, unto Thee do we cry: There is none holy as Thou, O Lord.
Ode 3 — Troparia
Deliver me from every temptation of the enemy, O holy Angel, and keep me from every evil and sinful action, and at the dreadful hour of death present my soul pure before God. Holy Guardian, enlighten me who am darkened by passions, raise me up who have fallen low, and make me worthy to serve my God for the days remaining to me.
Ode 4 — Troparia
O holy Angel, keeper of my life: pity me, a sinner who am consumed by many passions and transgressions, and deliver me from every assault of the enemy. Wherefore do I, a sinner, despise God's gifts? Why have I wasted the days given me in vanity and sin? O holy Guardian, pray to the Lord for my repentance.
Ode 5 — Irmos
I will cry unto Thee, O Lord my God, because Thou hast raised my life out of corruption, and dost save me from the deep abyss of sin.
Ode 6 — Troparia
O holy Guardian, preserve me in body and soul, and guard me from every snare of the enemy. With thy holy prayers present me at the dread Judgment without shame. O faithful Guardian of my soul, who art always present with me yet I know it not — intercede for me before God who loveth mankind, that all my sins may be blotted out.
Ode 7 — Irmos
The most holy children, in the midst of the furnace in Babylon, were not afraid of the fierce flame; but in the midst of the consuming fire they cried out and sang: Blessed art Thou, O Lord God of our Fathers.
Ode 8 — Troparia
O holy Angel, withdraw not thy mercy from me. Intercede for me before the Lord, that He may lead me in the ways of righteousness and preserve me from every temptation. I bless the Lord and praise and exalt Him above all forever. Holy Angel, intercede for me.
Ode 9 — Irmos
It is truly right to bless thee, O Theotokos, ever blessed and most blameless, and Mother of our God. More honorable than the Cherubim, more glorious beyond compare than the Seraphim, thou who without corruption gavest birth to God the Word, the very Theotokos, thee do we magnify.
Ode 9 — Troparia
O holy Guardian and keeper of my soul, grieve not over me any longer on account of my sins; but at the hour of my death appear to me as a comforter and guide, and lead my soul to the place of rest. O most holy Theotokos, thou who art the intercessor for all: intercede for me before thy Son and God, that He may grant me forgiveness of my sins and a Christian end.
Prayer to the Guardian Angel — After the Canon
O holy Angel of Christ, falling down before thee, I beseech and pray thee, my holy guardian appointed to govern and preserve my wretched soul and body: forgive me all my sins of this day by which I have sorely grieved thee, my holy guardian. Preserve me from every temptation of the evil one, that I may not anger God by any sin. Pray for me to the Lord, that He might confirm me in His fear, and make me a worthy servant of His goodness. Amen.
