The Four Gospels

60 days · The four Gospels

All four Gospels read in canonical order — Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John — over two months. The pace is roughly one chapter daily, slow enough to walk with our Lord through His earthly ministry, His Passion, and His Resurrection. Well-suited to inquirers encountering Christ in the witness of the apostles for the first time, and to the faithful returning to the Gospels with fresh attention. A short note accompanies each day to orient the reader.

This is a rule, not a race. Miss a day; begin again the next. The Church does not measure us by speed.

Opening Prayer Open my eyes, that I may behold wondrous things from Your law. Bless, O Lord, this rule of reading. Plant Your Word in my heart that I may not sin against You. Lead me, through this Scripture, to the parish where You are present in the Mysteries; to the Fathers who taught Your saints; to the priest who shepherds Your flock; and to You, the Word made flesh, our Savior Jesus Christ. Amen.

Closing Prayer Glory to You, O Lord, who has fed me from the storehouse of Your Word. What I have read, plant in my life. What I have not understood, send Your Holy Spirit to teach. What I have understood, give me grace to live. Bring me, by these readings, deeper into Your Holy Church, to the parish where Your Body is present in the Mysteries. For You are the Word, and apart from You there is no life. Amen.
