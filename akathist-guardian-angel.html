Akathist to the Guardian Angel

The Akathist Hymn to the Holy Guardian Angel — a deeply personal prayer to the heavenly intercessor appointed to each soul at baptism.

← Back to Prayer Corner
Akathist Hymn
Prayed privately at home, especially on Mondays (the day dedicated to the Angels in the weekly cycle) and in times of spiritual danger.
Every baptized Christian is given a Guardian Angel at the moment of their entry into the Church — a heavenly companion, intercessor, and protector for the entire life of the soul. This akathist is addressed personally to one's own Guardian Angel, asking for intercession, guidance, and accompaniment through life and at the moment of death. It is among the most intimate of all Orthodox prayers.
Kontakion 1
O chosen minister of God and guardian of my soul, I thy sinful servant offer thee this hymn of praise. Thou dost protect me day and night from every evil of soul and body, and dost bring me to God by thine intercessions. Wherefore, I cry to thee:
Rejoice, O Angel of God, my holy Guardian!
Oikos 1
Sent by the Lord as minister of His mercy, thou dost stand invisibly beside me all the days of my life, guarding my soul and body from every harm: Rejoice, messenger of God's love toward men; Rejoice, faithful protector of my soul! Rejoice, thou who dost guard me while I sleep; Rejoice, thou who dost watch over me in my waking hours! Rejoice, thou who dost accompany me on every journey; Rejoice, thou who art present with me in every place! Rejoice, thou who dost intercede for me before the throne of God; Rejoice, thou who dost never abandon me in my sinfulness!
Rejoice, O Angel of God, my holy Guardian!
Kontakion 2
Knowing that I am weak and prone to sin, thou dost watch over me with tender care, as a mother watches over her child. Thou dost grieve over my falls and rejoice over my repentance, ever presenting my soul before God who hears those who cry:
Alleluia!
Oikos 2
I know not, O holy Guardian, how great is thy care for me, nor how often thou hast preserved me from dangers seen and unseen. But I know that thou art ever at my side: Rejoice, thou who dost turn away from me the snares of the enemy; Rejoice, thou who dost lift me up when I have fallen! Rejoice, thou who dost inspire me to good thoughts; Rejoice, thou who dost kindle in me the love of God! Rejoice, thou who art present at my prayer; Rejoice, thou who dost bear my petitions before God!
Rejoice, O Angel of God, my holy Guardian!
Kontakion 3 — 12
[The full Akathist to the Guardian Angel continues through 13 kontakia and oikoi, addressing the angel's role as protector in temptation, comforter in sorrow, guide at the hour of death, and intercessor at the Last Judgment. The complete text is available in Orthodox prayer books.]
Kontakion 13
O holy Angel, accept this my humble prayer and intercede with God for me, a sinner, that He may forgive my transgressions and grant me to amend my life, and at my last hour, bring my soul safely to the heavenly Kingdom, where with thee and all the angels I may cry:
Alleluia!
Prayer to the Guardian Angel
O Angel of Christ, my holy Guardian and protector of my soul and body, forgive me all my sins of today, and deliver me from all the wiles of the enemy. May I not anger my God by any sin, and pray for me to the Lord, that He might confirm me in His fear and make me a worthy servant of His goodness. Amen.
